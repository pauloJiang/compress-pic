    What is image compression?

    Image compression reduces file size by removing redundant data or using efficient encoding—so images load faster while still looking sharp on screen. Whether you need smaller JPG photos, optimized PNG graphics, or lightweight WebP assets, compressjpg.pro handles every format in one place.

    How to compress images online

    Compress any supported image format in a few steps:

    1. Drag and drop your images into the upload area, or click Click to upload.
    2. Adjust Quality and Max width to balance size and clarity.
    3. Click Compress and compare before/after file sizes.
    4. Download optimized files when you are satisfied with the results.

    FAQ

    Is image compression safe?

    Yes. This tool runs entirely in your browser. Your images are not uploaded to a server, so you keep full control of your files.

    Does compression reduce image quality?

    Lossy formats like JPG may remove fine detail at lower quality settings. At moderate quality (around 70–85%), changes are usually invisible on screens.

    Can I compress images on mobile?

    Yes. The tool works on modern mobile browsers. Open the page, upload photos from your gallery, and download compressed files.

    All processing happens on your device. Nothing is uploaded to a server.
